Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: My device isn't showing up or connecting to Kopi. What should I do?

This is often due to the phone's connection settings.

  • Check your phone's USB connection mode:
    1. After connecting your phone to your computer via USB, swipe down from the top of your phone's screen to open the notification shade.
    2. Look for a notification related to "USB charging," "USB for," or "USB options." Tap on it.
    3. Select "File Transfer / MTP" (Media Transfer Protocol). Avoid "Charging only," "PTP," or "MIDI."
  • Reconnect the USB cable: Unplug and re-plug the USB cable from both your phone and your computer. Try different USB ports if available.
  • Restart Kopi: Sometimes a fresh start for the app can resolve connection glitches.
  • Restart your computer: As a last resort, restarting your computer can resolve underlying driver issues.

Q2: I have a Samsung phone and Kopi isn't connecting, even after selecting File Transfer mode.

Samsung phones sometimes require an extra step during the initial connection.

  • Look for the "Allow access to phone data" prompt: The very first time you connect your Samsung phone to a new computer, your phone should display a pop-up asking "Allow access to phone data?" You must tap "Allow" on your phone for MTP to work.
  • If you missed it: Unplug your phone, restart Kopi, and then reconnect your phone. Watch closely for the "Allow" prompt and tap it.

Q3: Kopi freezes or becomes unresponsive when I try to connect to my device.

This can happen occasionally due to timing or minor communication hiccups.

  • Restart Kopi: Close the app completely and open it again.
  • Reconnect your device: Disconnect your phone from the USB cable, wait a few seconds, and then plug it back in.
  • Try connecting in a different order: Sometimes, connecting the phone first and then opening the app, or vice-versa, can make a difference.

Q4: My phone is connected and charging, but I can't see my files in Kopi.

This is the most common MTP issue! Your phone is likely in "Charging only" mode.

  • Change your USB connection mode to "File Transfer":
    1. Swipe down from the top of your phone's screen to access notifications.
    2. Find the USB notification (it often says "Charging this device via USB").
    3. Tap this notification and select "File Transfer / MTP" from the options.
  • Once "File Transfer" is selected, Kopi should refresh and display your device's storage.
